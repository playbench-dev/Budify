Certain personal information are required in order to use the Budify Platform. This includes your legal name, e-mail address, phone number, nationality, date of birth, and profile photo. As you use Budify and its Payment Services, Budify may automatically collect personal information. Such information will be used mostly to enhance your experience on Budify’s platform, and may include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Location based on your IP address or GPS according to your device settings. If you decide to enable location information collection on your device, we may collect such information when you’re not directly using Budify services as well.

  • Payment and transaction information, such as payment method used, date and time, amount, expiration date, billing postcode, payment email address, IBAN information, address, and other transaction information.

We may collect your personal information from third party sources, such as:

Depending on the applicable laws and its permissions, Budify may receive additional information about you, such as demographic data or information and references in order to help detect safety issues and fraud from third party service providers and partners, and collaborate or combine information we have about you. 4. SHARING AND DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION

4.1 Sharing with Your Approval.

When you approve a third-party platform to access your Budify account or information, or participate in marketing activities from third-party platforms, Budify will share your information according to the level of your consent and authorization.

When allowed in accordance to applicable laws, Budify may use your information in anonymous forms, for the purpose of marketing, promotion, and social media platforms.

4.2 Sharing Between Budify Users.

Budify may share your information to facilitate and enable reservations, transactions, and interactions:

  • When a reservation or a dispute is submitted, information such as your profile, name, names of additional participants, cancellation history, dispute history, review information, age, and other data may be shared. When the reservation is confirmed, additional information that may help facilitate the experience, such as your contact information, phone number, profile photo, or health condition may be shared.
  • When you submit additional information at your own consent during a specific reservation, such as religion, gender, and health conditions, they will automatically be shared to your Buddy.
  • When you as a Buddy confirms a reservation, information such as your profile, legal name, phone number, photo, address, and contact information may be shared to the Guests.
  • When you as a Guest invite additional Guests to a reservation, your legal name, travel schedule, Buddy name, Buddy information, experience address and details may be shared to the additional Guests.

4.3 Information You Publish in Profiles, Experiences, and other Public Information.

You can make certain information visible to the public, which may also be visible through third-party search engines and platforms.

  • Your public profile page, including your name, profile photo, description, and location.
  • Your experience page, including your profile, photo, experience description and location, reservation availability, demand information, and other additional information.
  • Your reviews and ratings, including the contents and media you post.
  • Posts and comments in the Budify community, blog, and social media.

Budify may submit your information to legal courts, law enforcement, government authorities, tax authorities, or authorised third parties, when Budify is legally required and permitted to do so, or when it is reasonably necessary to do so:

  • Comply with legal requests or claims asserted against Budify.
  • Respond to a legal request related to criminal or illegal investigation.

In cases where Budify facilitates tax collection and payment and are legally required or permitted, Budify may disclose user information on transaction dates and amounts, reservations, tax payments and information, legal names, addresses, tax identification numbers, and contact information to the according tax authorities.

Budify may also provide other relevant and required information when Budify is obliged and permitted to submit your information to other legal, government, and public authorities.
